About Us The MetaSoft team presents:
ElixirLine

Who we are?

ElixirLine is a university startup dedicated to transforming the artisanal wine industry through technology. Founded by a team of Software Engineering students, we are passionate about helping small and medium-scale winemakers digitize and optimize their production processes.

What is our mission?

Our mission is to empower artisanal winemakers by providing a mobile platform that streamlines vineyard management, simplifies production tracking, and enhances decision-making — all while preserving the essence and tradition of handcrafted wine.

Subscriptions

ElixirLine offers flexible subscription plans tailored to the size and needs of your vineyard. Whether you're just starting or managing a larger operation, choose a plan that fits and scale up as you grow — pay only for what you need, when you need it.

Our Services

At ElixirLine, we connect people who need to move with available cars near them, offering a flexible, safe, and collaborative way to rent and generate income.

🔧 Task Planning & Scheduling

Organize and assign vineyard activities with a calendar-based system that adapts to seasonal workflows.

📲 Mobile Field Logging

Workers can record tasks, notes, and incidents directly from the field — even offline — with automatic sync.

🍷 Winemaking Process Tracking

Monitor each stage of the vinification process, from harvest to bottling, ensuring traceability and quality control.

📦 Inventory & Input Management

Track agricultural supplies, additives, and stock levels in real time to avoid shortages or overuse.

📈 Analytics & Production Reports

Access intuitive dashboards and reports to make informed decisions and spot improvement opportunities.

👥 User Roles & Access Control

Define roles for winemakers, field workers, and administrators with custom permissions for each.
